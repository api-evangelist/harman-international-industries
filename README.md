# Harman International

Harman International, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Samsung Electronics, is a global leader in connected technologies for automotive, consumer, and enterprise markets. Harman is best known for connected car systems (Ignite, Ready Connect), professional audio and video solutions, and consumer audio brands including JBL, Harman Kardon, AKG, Infinity, Mark Levinson, and Revel.

## APIs

Harman does not currently publish a public developer portal or open APIs. Integrations with Harman platforms are typically delivered through commercial partnerships, OEM agreements, and direct engagement with Harman's automotive, consumer, and professional business units.
